Objectives

The chapter is active again. Now the website needs to support it properly.

AMS Pre-college needs a cleaner and easier-to-manage website that helps Felix update content, collect Join Us responses correctly, and present the chapter in a more polished way.

What the website needs to achieve

1
Improve the homepage UI so the website looks better and feels more current.
2
Fix the Join Us page with a Google Form that routes responses to the right person.
3
Clarify the About section so visitors understand the chapter and its connection to AMS.
4
Set up an Events & News area Felix can update with dates, locations and attendance information.
5
Support transfer of website ownership so Felix can manage the site moving forward.

✓
